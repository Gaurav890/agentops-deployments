# AgentOps — AI Workflow Deployment Practice

I design and deploy AI agents into real organizations.
This repo documents what I build, what I learn, 
and what actually works in production.

---

## What This Is

Most AI projects fail not because the model was wrong.
They fail because nobody understood the workflow 
they were replacing.

AgentOps is my methodology for going into an 
organization, mapping their operations, identifying 
the highest-leverage AI automation opportunity, 
and deploying a working agent — measurably fast.

This is not a demo repo. Every deployment here 
happened in a real organization with real users.
