# Workflow Audit — v1

Questions I ask every organization before 
building anything.

## Operations
1. What does your team do more than 10x per week?
2. Where do things get stuck waiting for a 
   human decision?
3. What do new hires take longest to learn?

## Communication
4. What questions do you answer over and over 
   to the same people?
5. Where does information get lost between 
   teams or tools?

## Documents
6. What documents does your team reference 
   constantly but struggle to find answers in?
7. What reports do you generate manually 
   every week or month?

## Decisions
8. What decisions follow a clear pattern 
   but still require a human every time?
9. Where do errors happen most often and why?
10. If you could eliminate one repetitive task 
    tomorrow what would it be?
